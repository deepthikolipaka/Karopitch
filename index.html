About Karo Pitch

Built for Founders Across Bharat

Thousands of founders are building real, impactful businesses but struggle to access investors, mentorship, and visibility. Karo Pitch bridges that gap.

D2C Brands MSMEs Tech Startups Manufacturing Consumer Startups SaaS
"We don't just fund startups — we discover the builders others haven't found yet."
— KaroStartup Mission

Karo Pitch is a structured pitch platform modelled on closed-room investor events. Selected startups present their businesses to a curated audience of angels, VCs, and sector-specific investors. Our mission is to make funding accessible to every deserving founder — not just those in metro cities.

The Process

How Karo Pitch Works

A simple 4-step journey from application to funding — designed to be founder-friendly.

01
Apply with Your Pitch Deck
Submit your startup profile and pitch deck through our simple application form. No jargon, no gatekeeping.
02
Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup
Our team reviews every application. Shortlisted founders get notified and prepped for the pitch event.
03
Pitch Live to Investors
Present your business in a curated, high-energy pitch session in front of active investors and angels.
04
Raise Funding and Scale
Get matched with interested investors, close your round, and take your startup to the next level.
